How Verso works

p. 01

Read every page

Your PDF is opened locally using PDF.js, the same engine behind Firefox's built-in reader.

p. 02

Match the geometry

Page size, margins, indents and the gaps between words and lines are measured from the PDF, not reflowed — plus font, size, and bold or italic per run.

p. 03

Hand you a .doc

The result is packaged as a Word-compatible file, ready to open, edit, and reformat as you like.

Nothing you convert leaves your device. Mirrors page size, spacing & tables — images and columns still don't carry over.
